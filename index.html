Executive Summary & Gap Analysis

This section contrasts the operational bottlenecks currently restricting Vital Bone Wellness with the streamlined, automated future state proposed. Understanding these gaps is critical to validating the project's ROI.

⚠ Current State (Pain Points)

  • ✗ 5 Disjointed WordPress Sites: Fragmented management and inconsistent branding.
  • ✗ Manual Printing/Scanning: Bone density reports require heavy staff intervention and are easily lost.
  • ✗ High Call Volume: No self-service rescheduling leads to overwhelmed front desks.
  • ✗ Revenue Loss: Lack of automated follow-ups results in high no-show rates.

✓ Proposed State (The Solution)

  • ✓ 1 Unified WordPress Instance: Centrally managed, responsive, and location-aware.
  • ✓ DICOM/HL7 Edge-to-Cloud Sync: Automated extraction and secure upload of medical reports.
  • ✓ Self-Service Portal: "One-Click" rescheduling and document access for patients.
  • ✓ Card-on-File & SMS: Enforced financial commitment and active confirmation loops.

The "3-Track" Parallel Architecture

To deliver a massive operational shift within 6-8 weeks, we separate the project into three parallel technical workstreams. Click on each track below to explore the specific technical integrations and configurations being implemented.

Frontend & Widgets

Consolidating the disjointed web presence into a unified, high-conversion 'Front Door'. Designed for a non-technical staff to manage easily.

  • WordPress Consolidation Merge 5 legacy sites into one modern, responsive platform.
  • Mindbody Branded Web Inject native widgets for Schedule, Appointments, and Cart flows.
  • Location-Based Routing Dynamic JavaScript routing to load correct pricing/widgets based on user's selected clinic.
  • Legacy Device Testing Ensuring full functionality on the older iPads used by in-clinic staff.

Operational Impact: Achieving the 25% Reduction

Financial Commitment
